Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • ⅓ cup melted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup softened butter (for filling)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar (for icing)
  • 2 tbsp milk (for icing)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (for icing)

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add melted butter, egg, salt, and flour to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  4. Roll out the dough into a rectangle on a floured surface. Spread softened butter over the dough.
  5. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon, then sprinkle evenly over the buttered dough.
  6. Roll up the dough tightly from the long side and cut into 12 equal pieces.
  7. Place the rolls in a greased baking dish, cover, and let rise for another 30 minutes.
  8. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  9. While the rolls are baking, prepare the icing by mixing powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  10. Drizzle the icing over the warm cinnamon rolls before serving.
