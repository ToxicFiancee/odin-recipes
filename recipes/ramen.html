Ramen Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 1 liter chicken broth
  • 100g sliced pork belly
  • 2 boiled eggs
  • 1 cup sliced green onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, sliced

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat the sesame oil and saut√© the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken broth, soy sauce, and miso paste. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a separate pan, cook the sliced pork belly until crispy.
  5. To assemble, divide the noodles into bowls, pour the hot broth over them, and top with sliced pork belly, boiled eggs, and green onions.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
